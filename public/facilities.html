Campus Facilities

State-of-the-art Infrastructure

We provide a wide array of premium facilities to ensure our students have a comfortable and enriching campus life.

Central Digital Library

A massive 4-story automated library containing over 500,000 physical volumes, seamless access to leading IEEE digital journals, private study pods, and 24/7 dedicated high-speed Wi-Fi zones.

Smart AI & Robotics Labs

Fully equipped multi-disciplinary laboratories utilizing the latest industry tech. Spaces designed for advanced computing, embedded logic, 3D printing, and rapid prototyping capabilities.

Premium Accommodations

Modern residential halls with AC/Non-AC options, attached pristine bathrooms, fast internet connectivity, strict 24-hour smart security, and lush recreational zones for a home-like experience.

Olympic-Grade Sports Arena

Extensive sports facilities including an indoor multi-purpose gymnasium, a fully filtered swimming pool, synthetic tennis courts, and large well-maintained football and cricket grounds.

Multi-Cuisine Food Courts

Hygienic, vibrant, and spacious eating hubs serving diverse nutritional, multi-cuisine meals including vegetarian, vegan, and organic selections sourced from local fresh farms.

Grand Auditorium

A spectacular 2000-seater fully air-conditioned auditorium with premium acoustics and massive digital screens, designed perfectly to host seminars, cultural festivals, and international summits.
